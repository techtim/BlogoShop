About us

Well, first of all i should mention that in the Khokhlovskiy Lane, 7., you can find an art-centre which is located in an old building which itself is quite a story and a piece of Moscow architecture.

It was built in XVII cent, and it used to be a living quarters for a russian diplomat. Next, in XVIII cent. there was the archive of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. It was, basically, a big library with capacity to issue books.

Famous russian poet, Alexander Pushkin, used to visit this place very often. In XIX cent. it was bought by a person who used the facility to issue music notes, later he became the biggest music notes publisher in Russia.

For example, Chaikovskiy was first officially published here, in Khokhlovskiy, 7. Later, the publishing house was closed and the building was abandoned for several years until some rooms were rented by young artists who used them as workshops.

We have a shop here, in Khokhlovskiy, 7. It provides mostly clothes made by outstanding Russian designers (though we also have some jewelery in stock). We surely follow the trends - but we don’t sell popular brands, we focus on local items.

We have discovered lot of independent talents. Stylistically our consumer is into smart casual clothing. People who buy from us can be called creative class of Moscow, they combine business and leisure time within a day.

We carefully choose the people we work with - and as the result we receive very special collections of fine quality. In our shop one can find unique pieces of clothing and accessories that you won't find anywhere else. More than that, we’ve got an online shop and a blog.
